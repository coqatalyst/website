## Introduction

CoQatalyst is a teen organizations made for other teens who want to get into rigorous research, high schoolers who don't want to wait till college, impatient scientists who want to discover and explore in the direction thier curiosity takes them.

## The Experiment

This organization is kind of an experiment, an experiment to improve the status of the R&D output of India and help like-minded teens connect, when high schoolers who are interested in the field learn from the start, they get an advantage, INDIA and the WORLD gets an advantage and thus to connect all research oreinted high schoolers, We have started this!!! 

## Method

We don't except anyone 14-year-old to know how how research or actual science is done, but we believe in teaching them this by healthy competition.
We host exhibitions, competitions and meetups based around STEM and AI research for teens so that they can discuss, connect and collaborate.
**We run our discord so distance is not an issue everybody can help anybody.**

## Conclusion

If you are interested in research, STEM, Machine Learning or AI or even want to volunteer with us reach our goal, join our discord and becam a part of the community.
